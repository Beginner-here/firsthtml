by Stan Lee

Starring :

  1. Iron man
  2. Captain America
  3. Thor
  4. Hulk
  5. Hawkeye
  6. Black widow

At a glance

  • Iron man :Tony Stark is a Genius, Billionaire, Playboy, Philanthropist.
  • Captain America :Steve Rogers is a kid from Brooklyn who took a serum and became a super soldier.
  • Thor : Thor ,Son of Odin is the most powerful Avenger who is from Asgard.
  • Hulk : Bruce Banner is a science student who got affected by gamma radiation and turned into a superhuman.
  • Hawkeye : Agent Clint is a bow and arrow specialist
  • Black widow : Natasha Romanoff is a specialised agent.
